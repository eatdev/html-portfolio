The Best Movies According to Drew

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time

A Christmas Story

Christmas is my favorite time of year. And this movie is an absolute must. I don't only watch it once, I watch it many times!

Halloween

Halloween is my second favorite holiday! The Halloween movie franchise is my favorite set of movies to watch during the spooky season. I watch them all!

Alien

Outside of horror and Christmas, I do love a good sci-fi movie! This is one of my favorites. It gave me nightmares as a child.
